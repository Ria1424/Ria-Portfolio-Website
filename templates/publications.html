Worker Detection, Tracking and Efficiency Analysis in Factories

This paper presents an automated framework to monitor industrial environments. By applying advanced computer vision models, specifically YOLOv8 for real-time detection and ByteTracker for multi-object tracking, the system tracks worker movements, quantifies operational efficiencies, and enforces safety compliance parameters within factory floor layouts.

Does Punctuation Matter? An Empirical Analysis of Sentiment Tools

An empirical investigation assessing the impact of structural text punctuation on sentiment classification results. The research benchmarks multiple leading Natural Language Processing (NLP) models, comparing transformer-based architectures (BERT) against lexicon and rule-based pipelines (VADER and Text-blob) to evaluate the statistical significance of punctuation in sentiment accuracy.
